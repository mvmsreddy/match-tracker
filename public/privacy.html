Privacy Policy — Match Tracker Pro

Last updated: 2026-07-21

Match Tracker Pro ("the app") is a tennis match-tracking and tournament app. This page explains what information the app collects, how it's used, and how you can control it.

Information we collect

  • Account information — your name and email address, either entered directly at sign-up or provided by Google if you use "Continue with Google."
  • Profile information (optional) — details you choose to add such as AITA registration number, date of birth, gender, state, club name, ranking, and bio, used to enter tournaments and personalize your dashboard.
  • Match and practice data — everything you log while tracking a match: player names, point-by-point results, scores, shot types, notes, and match duration. This is the core data the app exists to store for you.
  • Tournament and draw data — if you or your organizer uses the tournament module: entries, draws, schedules, and results.
  • Location (optional, one-time) — only if you tap "Get Weather" while logging a match, the app requests your device's location once to look up current conditions from the Open-Meteo weather service. This coordinate is sent to Open-Meteo for that single request and is not stored by us or tied to your account.

How we use this information

Solely to operate the app: authenticating you, saving and displaying your matches and stats, running tournament draws and schedules, and (optionally) showing current weather for a match. We do not use your data for advertising, and we do not sell or rent it to anyone.

Where your data is stored

Match, profile, and tournament data are stored in a Postgres database hosted by Supabase, protected by row-level security so only you (and, where a feature explicitly allows it, your linked coach or tournament organizer) can access your records. All traffic between the app and our backend is encrypted in transit (HTTPS).

Third parties

  • Supabase — database and authentication hosting.
  • Google — only if you choose "Continue with Google" to sign in.
  • Open-Meteo — receives a one-time GPS coordinate only when you tap "Get Weather"; see above.

We do not share your data with any other third party.

Data retention and deletion

You can delete individual matches at any time from Match History within the app. To delete your entire account and all associated data, contact us at the email below and we will remove it.

Children's privacy

The app is used by junior tennis players, often with a parent or coach managing the account on their behalf. We do not knowingly collect personal information from a child without the involvement of a parent, guardian, or coach creating and managing that account. If you believe a child has provided information to us without appropriate involvement, contact us and we will remove it.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date above will be revised. Continued use of the app after a change means you accept the updated policy.
